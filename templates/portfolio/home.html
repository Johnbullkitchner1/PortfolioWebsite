About Me

I’m Kitchner Johnbull, a passionate Computer Science graduate from Federal University Ndufu-Alike Ikwo (FUNAI), based in Abuja, Nigeria. With a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end development, I specialize in crafting scalable, user-friendly web applications. My journey began with hands-on experience at companies like Edifel Designs and Eztech, where I honed my skills in React.js, Nest.js, and Tailwind CSS. I thrive on solving complex problems, building responsive designs, and integrating dynamic features to enhance user experiences.

Beyond coding, I enjoy exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects on GitHub, and staying active with hobbies like football and sprinting. My goal is to leverage my expertise to deliver innovative solutions that drive business growth and user satisfaction.
